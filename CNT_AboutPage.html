Welcome to Thole Group

We are a fifth-generation family business established in the 1920s

Nanuram Fandulal

NF is the oldest company in the group started in 1921 and primarily trades in jaggery, sugar, pulses, and seeds in the domestic market. It operates from the Kopargaon taluka located in the Ahmednagar district of Maharashtra .

Sona Poly Plast

Established in 1981, Sona Poly has become a renowned brand for drip and sprinkler irrigation systems with a vast dealer and distributor network in Maharashtra. It is now rapidly expanding in Karnataka, Madhya Pradesh, Chhattisgarh, Tamil Nadu, Gujarat and Rajasthan.

Anand Pharma

Anand Pharma is involved in the distribution of pharmaceutical products and has a strong network of retailers in the Ahmednagar and Nashik districts of Maharashtra .

SG Vidyalaya

SG Vidyalaya School, established in 1934, is a not-for-profit initiative of the Thole Group which today runs four schools in Kopargaon providing free of cost education to students

CNT Global Link

CNT Global Link was born out of a deep-rooted commitment to empower Indian farmers and to contribute to the nation's growth through the potent avenue of exports. Leveraging the rich agricultural heritage of India, we chose to specialize in the export of herbs and spices, products that encapsulate India's unique edge in the global market.

The credibility and respect earned by the Thole Group over nearly a century of successful enterprise form the backbone of CNT Global Link. Our affiliation with the Thole Group not only provides us with a robust network across India but also instills trust in our partners. This good will enables us to source the finest quality Herbs and Spices from different regions of the country. Our strong sourcing capabilities, coupled with our commitment to quality and service, put us in an exceptional position to meet our clients' demands reliably and consistently. At CNT Global Link, we're not just about fulfilling your needs, we're about exceeding your expectations every time.
